# Introduction and Disclaimer

In this post, I will jot down the tips I gathered during my Ph.D. life, which faithfully records the small tips that I found helpful. And take care, all the tips only fit ME well, not the reader.

# Write down your crazy thoughts

Jot it down as soon as possible whenever something comes to your mind. You will decide whether it is valuable or not later. If the answer is no, cross out similar ideas in the future.

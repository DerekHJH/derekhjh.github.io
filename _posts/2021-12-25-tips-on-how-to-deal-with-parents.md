# Introduction and Disclaimer

In this post, I will jot down the tips I gathered during the time with my parents, which faithfully records the feelings, dos, and don'ts when dealing with them. And take care, all the tips only fit ME well, not the reader.

# Talk over the phone

Never talk with your father on the phone for over ten minutes, and never transform a family talk into a pathetic argument on something technical. For example, talking about your humble point of view of economics is a bad idea. Because frequently, you will get retorted and even "lectured." Always remember that it is time-wasting and feeling-wasting to persuade. 

Short answers to each of their stupid and so-called "caring" questions are strongly recommended. Being short does not mean being curt. At least you should provide something they have never heard of about yourself. Maybe you can talk about the exciting things you have done in school. But stop when you are done; no comments below, no comments, please.
